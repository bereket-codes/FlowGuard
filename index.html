About FlowGuard

FlowGuard was built on a simple observation: most people don't struggle to earn money — they struggle to direct it the moment it arrives. The gap between receiving income and spending it carelessly is where financial health is won or lost.

We don't track what you already spent. We help you decide what to do before you spend it. Every dollar gets a job. Every category gets intention. That's the FlowGuard promise.
